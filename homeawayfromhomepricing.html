Programs/Services

At Home Away from Home we understand the working parents need for reliable and high quality child care.

Therefore, we operate

7 days a week from 6:30am – 10:00pm.

The following are session times children may be enrolled into:

1. Full time AM session Monday – Friday 6:30am – 6:00pm

2. Part time AM session select 2 days Monday – Friday 6:30am – 6:00pm

3. Full time PM session Monday – Friday 2:00pm – 10:00pm

4. Part time PM session select 2 days Monday – Friday 2:00pm – 10:00pm

5. Weekend services (Saturday & Sunday) flexible times (6-hour maximum) AM tuition includes breakfast, lunch, and snack.

PM tuition includes afternoon snack, dinner, and bedtime snack.
