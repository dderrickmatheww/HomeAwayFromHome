Hello! My name is Cyasia J Zackery,


I am a twenty-six year old mother of one! I am originally from Pennsylvania, I relocated to Savannah three years ago after graduating from The Pennsylvania State University with an Associate degree in Human Development and Family Studies. More recently, I graduated from Southern Crescent Technical College with a Certificate in Early Childhood Program Administration. Children have always been a passion of mine and I have over eight years of professional child care experience. My past job titles include Paraprofessional, Lead day care teacher, Therapeutic Staff Support, and Babysitter. What I love most about working with children is helping and watching them grow and learn. My natural creativity and fun personality always makes my experiences with children unique and special.
